# South Korea

## General Information

South Korea, officially the Republic of Korea, is located in East Asia on the southern part of the Korean Peninsula. The capital city is Seoul. The official language is Korean, and the national currency is the South Korean Won (KRW).

### Key Facts

* Capital: Seoul
* Population: approximately 52 million people
* Official Language: Korean
* Currency: South Korean Won (KRW)
* Time Zone: UTC+9
* Government Type: Presidential Republic

## Major Cities

### Seoul

The capital and largest city of South Korea. Seoul serves as the political, economic, and cultural center of the country.

### Busan

The largest port city and the second-largest city by population.

### Incheon

An important transportation and economic hub known for Incheon International Airport.

### Daegu

A major industrial city with a strong manufacturing sector.

### Daejeon

A leading center for science, research, and technology.

## Economy

South Korea has one of the largest economies in the world and is known for its advanced technology and manufacturing industries.

Major South Korean companies include:

* Samsung
* LG
* Hyundai
* Kia

Key economic sectors:

* Electronics
* Automotive Industry
* Shipbuilding
* Information Technology
* Chemical Industry

## Education

South Korea has one of the most developed education systems in the world.

Leading universities include:

## Transportation

South Korea has a highly developed transportation infrastructure.

Major transportation systems include:

* Urban metro systems
* KTX high-speed rail
* Intercity bus network
* International airports

The largest airport is Incheon International Airport.

## Climate

South Korea has a temperate monsoon climate.

### Spring

March to May.

### Summer

June to August, generally hot and humid.

### Autumn

September to November.

### Winter

December to February, cold and dry.

South Korea is a global leader in technology and innovation.

Important characteristics:
